Guidelines for attending holy mass online
✝ Start preparing for holy mass before-hand. do not rush .
✝ Set-up a simple altar with a crucifix & a candle in your table near the device (mobile phone, tablet, laptop, tv) streaming the holy mass.
✝ Wear appropriate attire (do not attend the mass in your leisure wear or home wear). be serious in your preparation.
✝ Turn off other online devices & do not do anything else while you are participating in the holy mass. there should be nothing to distract your attention on the mass.
✝ Recite the corresponding responses to the prayers in the holy mass.
✝ Observe the proper gestures of standing, kneeling & sitting at appropriate times in the holy mass.
✝ During the time of receiving communion, please recite the spiritual communion prayer.
